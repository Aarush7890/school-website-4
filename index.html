The final cricket match in New Zeland
Won the final cricket match in New Zeland.

Budding cricketer from Apeejay School, Noida, Nirmalya Nath (Class X A) participated in the New Zealand Cricket Tour 2018 from October 25, 2018 to November 6, 2018, organised by Alien Sports Management after a rigorous selection process by their Coach. His team played a total of six matches, 5 of them in Auckland and 1 in Hamilton with teams of North Harbor Cricket Academy, Westlake Boys and Kings College from Auckland and Hamilton Boys High School, Hamilton. During the tour, Nirmalya acquired more skills in the game and most importantly he learnt their way of playing the beautiful game. He also got an insight into the culture and lifestyle of New Zealand. Nirmalya has received a lifetime membership from the North Shore Cricket Club, Devonport (NSCC).
